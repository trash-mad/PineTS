## When Do You Need a Commercial License?

The AGPL-3.0 license requires that any application using PineTS — whether distributed or offered as a network service — must also be released under the AGPL-3.0. This includes making your full source code available.

A commercial license removes the copyleft obligations and allows you to:

- Use PineTS in **proprietary / closed-source** applications
- Distribute products containing PineTS **without publishing your source code**
- Offer SaaS or hosted services built on PineTS **without AGPL-3.0 disclosure requirements**
- Receive **priority support and consultation**
